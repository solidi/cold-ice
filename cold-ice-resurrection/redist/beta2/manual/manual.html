Overview


      Cold-Ice Resurrection is a modification for the game, Half-Life. Its idea is based on the old modification for Half-Life called Cold-Ice. It is a extreme deathmath modification for those who are sick and tried of realistic teamplay. Cold-Ice offers many new features to deathmatch not seen before. Such as the totally new damage system. Instead of a general health count, Cold-Ice takes it one step further and offers a multi-part damage system. Cold-Ice offers many new weapons to kill with. Including many explosive, high damage weapons. Even some weapons may also affect its user such as an explosive sharpnel or explosion.


Installation


      Cold-Ice Resurrection is very easy to install. When you finish downloading the installation file from the internet (no, you didn't pay for this, did you?), double click on the installation file to begin. If you didn't install Half-Life in the default directory, make sure the root directory ponts to the half-life/ folder. After it finished, and it didn't expereince any problems, Boot up Half-Life and choose the custom games submenu, you should see "Cold-Ice Resurrection" in the window. This confirms that you have completed the simple task. Cold-Ice Resurrection is ready to go!


Getting Started


      Getting started with Cold-Ice Resurrection is rather simple and easy to the advid Half-Life player. After installation, boot up Half-Life with a shortcut -game cir so that Half-Life begins in CIR mode. If you do not know how to do this, you can simply enable Cold-Ice Resurrection by booting Half-Life and selecting CIR through the custom game menu. After this is completed, you must bind the controls through the controls menu section. There are new binds and changes from the standard HLDM, so please take the time to bind! Lastly, go into the customize submenu and select certain new optins and also change the default name to one of your own. And lets not forget about the new player models! Once all this is completed, its your job to find a CIR server and start a holy war!


The Weapons


      Cold-Ice Resurrection has a wide assortment of weapons at your disposal. All have their pros and cons and should be used wisely. The key to win is in the usage of the weapon. The weapons are assorted by melee, handguns, machineguns & rifles, explosive and handthrown. They are:

Melee

Steel Pipe - This weapon is used when you are fighting at close combat with your enemy. If it is needed, the steelpipe can get the job done. Its very strong but it opens you for easy attack of any other weapon class, especially rifles. The steelpipe should be used only when you either out of ammo or when you see a disabled victim. Smack their brains out!

Handguns

9mm Pistol - The simplest in handgun's 101. Its a pistol and it fires a 9mm bullet. It has stopping power if your good with the gun. When it comes down to speed and accuracy, look for no other. Do not under estimate its power, the pistol destroys the ignorant.

Machine Guns & Rifles

9mm Sub Machine Gun - Firing 30 bullets within 3 seconds, when it comes down to mass killing, this is the one for you. Its stopping power is great but only when used wisely. Do not get to comfortable with this tool, you will go through loads of rounds within seconds and burn a hole in you reserve. Note: Second fire selects different fire modes for each situtation at hand. Full-auto, burst and semi-atuo.

12g Slug Rifle - Commonly known as a "shotgun", this bad boy has the "not so marksman" accuracy to stop many men at a time. Although its pellets do not hurt as much as some of the other guns, its focus is on quanity. Blast away at close range and its all over for the enemy. Blast away at far range and its pretty much useless. The key to master this weapon is in the timing.

5.56mm Machine Gun - Automatic at its best, this baby fires 5.56mm rounds that pirece through walls and hurt the enemy to the fullest. The rifle also has a scope for those zoom in kills. Although it is slower than the 9mm sub machine gun, it will always get the job done. The scope zooms in 50m.

7.62 Blaser Rifle - German sniper hunting rifle. One the the sweetest features on this weapon is the no cock bolt system. Its design allows to eject shells without all that twisting and turning, saving time. The round, 7.62mm is very powerful and is made to kill. Shoot at any part of the body and its almost certain to destroy its health. Its up to you which way you want your victim dead!

762 Minigun - The only weapon in the CIR arsenal to change your victim into menced meat within seconds. Pumping out lots of shells in a short period of time, you have the capacity to kill crowds of players plus everything else thats in your way. But all this action comes at a price, you can't jump, run or even walk fast. The when the gun gets rolling, things get bloody!

Explosive and Handthrown

35mm Grenade Launcher - The best way to hurl explosive goodness at your foes is to use this bad-ass weapon. Packing one large barrel of 35mm grenades, theres no stopping you. Yet, it comes at a price, it has a heavy recoil which will blow you back acouple of meters. Fear not! You can deliever 6 contact greandes at your enemies within 6 seconds. Talk about "Hot and fresh out of the oven!"

C4 explosive - Ever wanted to "blow shit up with style?" Well, here is your chance. This explosive material is equipped with two options. A time fuse and remote controlled. The blast is very powerful and is likely to kill people in its explosion raduis. Use the option deploy wisely, or you will pay the price. Be careful, this bomb doesn't know friend from foe!

The Hud


      New in Cold Ice Resurrection is the completely rebuilt heads up display. This new feature will probably be the first thing that you will notice. What makes this hud so special is that it is 24bit and uses the vgui engine. The hud itself shows the following:

· Frags
· Deaths
· Clip
· Reserve
· Time
· Wins (under certian game modes)
· Loss (under certian game modes)
· Rune (under certain conditions)
