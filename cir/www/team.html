team - last update 1/23/01

[The Team] Nerds behind the whole mess :)

     Welcome to the team page, this is to identify the people behind the project. We are a bunch of talented creators that just want to create a fun and intresting modification.

Main Team

Surreal - The lead behind the project, and programming expert. He is the also the founder of Cold-Ice Resurrection. In his past he has founded the Half-Life Pro and Cold-Ice mod. He has also programmed for the modifications Wasteland HL and Trinity Command. He also has skills in the 2d department and somewhat in the mapping area.

Infinity - A main contributor, bringing his awesome modelling skills to Cold-Ice Resurrection (including his skinning abilities). He is also very skilled in the 2d department. He also has ability with mapping. Without him, this project would have never of come to existance.

Mapping Team

Panic - Panic boosts his mad mapping skills. Panic has years of mapping behind him; his unique mapping style brings life to the cir world. Also crowned lead mapper to this project.

Apocalypso - Apo (for short) is another woinderful mapper for this project. Apo is well known around this community. He is a part time mapper, only because he has dedicated work to the Holy Wars Mod. He is also a drunken bastard :).

Devious - He is another mapper for Cold-Ice Resurrection. His map, the mill, will most likely become our main-stream map because it fits CIR so damn well. When he has a plan, he executes it well.

Cyrus258 - Our newest mapper, Cryus, is a well spoken guy that has the skills to deliever. Cyrus enjoys deathmatch as much as Surreal does. But there is one thing that all of us can't figure out... what does 258 mean?
