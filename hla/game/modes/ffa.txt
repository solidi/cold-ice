Arena is FFA!
Free for All

Mutators:
(weapon): all weapons are changed to this and ammo changed to similar ammo.
anticamper: recieve damage if camping.
instagib: one shot gibs.
fatboy: the higher the frag / death streak, the bigger you are.
earning: earn weapons at certain frag amounts.
startfull: start fully loaded.
gore: increase in blood and guts.
