Iceman Arena

The Iceman is the enemy with lots of armor and weapons.
Commandos must destroy the Iceman within a limited amount of time.
Iceman must destroy all Commandos.

Iceman wins a point if dispatching all Commandos before time is up.
If the Iceman survives, the commando who doles the most damage wins a round.
Round ends in a draw if no damage is doled to Iceman when time is up.
