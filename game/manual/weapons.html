WEAPONS

 

Weapons that appear in the Version 1.75.

 

Combat Knife- This weapon is manual, just like the crowbar. It is way faster then the crowbar,
but does less damage.
Crowbar- Another manual weapon. It is slower than the knife, but does alot more damage.
Good Wrench- Another manual weapon that will do major damage to
any enemy, but is slowest out of all of the manual weapon family.

PPK- This weapon is handgun/semi-automatic, but has a slow brust rate. The secondary fire
adds a 5 inch silencer to the barrel and makes the gun silent.
Mag 60 automatic hangun- A fast automatic gun with 2 firing rates. This gun may have a fire
brust but its accuracy isnt great.
M-16- A assault rifle that any player will start off with. It has a clip of 30 5.56mm bullets.
This weapon only has a primary fire.

Chaingun- This automatic weapon is a beast throwing machine. Even though its bullets are weak,
you can easily pump a player with 50 bullets in 5 seconds. Its secondary fire is a slow
paced buckshot fire.
Sawed off Shotgun- A stanard automatic shotgun that has its barrel chopped in half. Its spread is
very large, but the pellets loose their power. The secondary fire has explosive buckshots to
burn holes through the players head 8).
Sniper Rifle- A scoped sniper rifle with power 7.65mm rounds.
Its clip and brust fire is very small, but one hit.. and its all over. The seconday fire is a zoom
with green night vision for those dark areas.

LAW Rocket Launcher- This automatic weapon Blasts 4 laser targeted 50 rockets at a player.
This weapon is excellent out in the open, but in a closed area, the late ignition on the rockets will
cause them to fly up into the ceiling.
Assult Cannon- This automatic cannon shoots large explosive shells on any player. Even though
the cannon only carries 6 shells, 2 direct hits and its all over.
Grenade Launcher- This powerful weapon throws 120 pound grenades at the enemy. Its primary
fire chucks contact grenades and the secondary fire chucks 2 second delay grenades at the
enemy
USAS-12 Auto Shotgun - This powerful weapon throws 10 buckshot slugs per
second at a enemy. This will probably be the most powerful weapon in Cold Ice. No image yet.

6- Pack Cluster Grenades- This powerful weapon is a time grenade packed with 6 other
grenades inside. Its very powerful but also deadly to its owner
Female Assassin Decoy Bombs- These sexy females are decoys for highly expolsive remote
bombs. Dont let her beauty fool you.
Kamikaze Dynamite Vest- This weapon of mass destruction is known to blow up people into tiny pieces of meat.
Be careful of the people with a red vest on.. they want you to go with them together.
Chumtoad- This little creature was pulled for the original game HL, but now its back and meaner
ever. Once he spots his vicitim, he chases the fresh meat and never lets go.


Theres alot of weapons to choose from! Choose correctly and you will come out the winner.
